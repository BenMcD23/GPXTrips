Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is GPXTrips?
A: At GPXTrips, we specialize in providing a powerful SaaS platform tailored for outdoor enthusiasts, athletes, and travelers. Our platform allows users to upload GPX files – a popular file format for GPS data – and gain valuable insights into their journeys.

Q: My account has been disabled. What do I do?
A: Contact Support: If you're unable to determine the reason for the account disablement or if you believe it was done in error, please contact our developers team immediately.
Q: Can I use my account on multiple devices?
A: Yes, you can use your account on multiple devices. However, you can only be logged in on one device at a time.
